Frequently Asked Questions :

Can I customize my cake order?

Yes, cakes and cupcakes can be decorated with edible images, inscriptions and themed sugar decons. The image is edible ink printed on top of rice paper that adheres to the icing and inscriptions are in butter cream. To place a custom order, call your local bakery

What type of custom orders can you do?

Our team will work closely with you to create custom cakes, cupcakes, favors, dessert tables and more. To request additional information, please call or email one of our event coordinators at 111-222-333 or email one of our local event coordinators at: info@theicing.com. to place a custom order.

Do you make vegan cakes or desserts?

Yes! In addition to vegan chocolate and vegan vanilla cake we do have an ever- expanding list of vegan baked goods, including cookies, muffins, scones, and other delicious treats. Please contact us to see what we have to offer as this list changes quickly.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend that you call the bakery to place your order at least 48 hours in advance. We can usually accommodate most orders, however, the more notice you give us the more likely we will be able to fulfill your order.

What is your pick-up/delivery policy?

We are happy to deliver your cake. For large, multi-tiered cakes we insist on delivering and setting up the cake ourselves to ensure safe transport and best results. Smaller cakes may be picked up or delivered. Pickup and delivery times will be arranged when the cake is ordered.
