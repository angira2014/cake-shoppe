    • How can I cancel my order?

    Orders must be cancelled more than 24 hours of the preferred delivery date and before 2:00 PM CST on that day. Please call 111-222-333 so that we may assist you with your request.

    Should I refrigerate my cake or cupcakes?

    No, that will dry them out. We specialize in old-fashioned baked goods, made from scratch and baked in small batches throughout the day, using only the finest ingredients. Our product is made without preservatives and should be enjoyed on the day received. If you plan on storing them overnight, tightly wrap the box in plastic wrap and leave at room temperature.

    Do you have any gluten-free items?

    Our flourless chocolate cake, mini meringues and lace meringues are gluten-free.

    Where can I find the nutritional information for your cupcakes?

    We use a variety of premium ingredients in our cupcakes and icings, including milk, cream, butter, eggs, sugar and flour. Our cupcakes do not contain any preservatives or trans fats in either the cake or icing. Currently, we do not have any other nutritional information available for review.
